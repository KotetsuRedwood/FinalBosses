--==What is a Digital Root?==--
~~play 9 Hours, 9 Persons, 9 Doors~~

To find the digital root of a set of numbers,
add all of the numbers together. Then, if the
result has more than 1 digit, continue until
you get a single digit.

--==WHY a Digital Root?==--

Seriously, play 9 Hours, 9 Persons, 9 Doors.
